# Determination of Refractive Index by Brewster Angle Method

**Brewster's angle** (also known as the polarization angle) is the angle of incidence at which light with a particular polarization is **perfectly transmitted** through a transparent dielectric surface, with no reflection. When unpolarized light is incident at this angle, the light that is reflected from the surface is perfectly polarized. â€” _Wikipedia_
