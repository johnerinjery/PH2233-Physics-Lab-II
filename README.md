# PH2233 Physics Lab II

PH2233 is a Physics Lab course offered at IISER Pune for the January 2026 semester. Course instructors are Drs. Umakant D. Rapol, Ushasi Roy and Aparna Deshpande. I will include, if time permits, a short wiki about each experiment as well.

### List of Experiments

1. Brewster’s Angle
2. Michelson Interferometer
3. Thermal expansion
4. Specific heat
5. Stefan’s law
6. Lee’s method (Thermal conductivity)
7. Thermistor
8. Rydberg’s constant
9. Photoelectric effect
10. Millikan’s oil drop

For this January 2026 Semester, the course evaluation is 35% for both Endsem and Midsem practical exams, 20% for lab note and viva (by faculty) and 10% for lab note and viva (By TA).
